Educational Aide for an Amazing Kid

We're hiring 1–2 caring, curious people to support our 8-year-old son at Cottonwood Day School. No prior experience required.

Position Details

  • Schedule Monday–Thursday, 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM (~32 hrs/week)
  • Openings 1–2 aides (two 2-day positions preferred, or one 4-day role)
  • Start Date Paid summer training hours, then staff orientation in early August
  • School Begins Week of August 17th
  • Location Cottonwood Day School, Bozeman, MT

About the Role

Our son is a joyful, curious, and determined 8-year-old who loves movement, creative play, and hands-on learning. Due to a prenatal neurological injury, he has challenges with vision processing, working memory, and executive function. With the right support, he thrives — academically and socially.

Cottonwood Day School is a warm, positive, and deeply supportive place to work. This is a meaningful role with the opportunity to make a real difference in a child's daily confidence and growth.

Who We're Looking For

No prior teaching or therapeutic experience required. We're looking for someone who brings:

  • Genuine excitement for learning and a patient, positive attitude
  • Respect for his independence while providing gentle structure and support
  • Creativity and flexibility to help him access the curriculum
  • Warmth, reliability, and strong collaboration skills

Key Responsibilities

  • Pre-teach and post-teach lessons in one-on-one pull-out sessions
  • Support him in the classroom to help him fully access instruction
  • Adapt and prepare engaging, accessible learning materials
  • Use manipulatives and multisensory approaches to strengthen understanding
  • Maintain a simple daily log of the school day
  • Participate in regular check-ins with teachers and parents

Depending on experience · Paid summer training hours included
