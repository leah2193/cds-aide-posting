Educational Aide
2026–2027 School Year

We are seeking one or two dedicated Educational Aides to support our son at Cottonwood Day School. No prior teaching or therapeutic experience required.

Position Details

  • Schedule Monday–Thursday, 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM (~32 hrs/week)
  • Openings 1–2 positions (two 2-day roles preferred, or one 4-day role)
  • Summer Paid training hours at our home in Belgrade prior to school start
  • Start Date Staff orientation in early August; school begins August 17th
  • Location Cottonwood Day School, Bozeman, MT
  • Compensation $22.50 – $23.50 / hr depending on experience

About the Role

My son is an enthusiastic, determined 8-year-old. Due to a prenatal neurological injury, he has challenges with vision processing, general processing, working memory, and executive function. With structured, attentive support, he performs well academically and socially.

The Aide will work closely with his classroom teachers at Cottonwood Day School — a well-regarded, highly supportive learning environment — to ensure he has consistent access to instruction and curriculum throughout the school day.

What We're Looking For

We value attitude and character above credentials. Strong candidates will bring:

  • A patient, positive, and professional approach to working with children
  • Respect for his independence alongside a commitment to structure and routine
  • Adaptability and creativity in supporting access to the curriculum
  • Reliability and clear communication with teachers and parents

Responsibilities

  • Pre-teach and post-teach lessons during one-on-one pull-out sessions
  • Support him in the classroom to help him fully access instruction
  • Adapt and prepare learning materials to meet his needs
  • Use manipulatives and multisensory methods to reinforce understanding
  • Maintain a daily log of the school day
  • Participate in regular check-ins with teachers and parents
